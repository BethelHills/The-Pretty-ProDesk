WHAT ARE COOKIES?

Cookies are small data files stored on your device when you visit a website. They help improve functionality, user experience, and website performance.

The Pretty ProDesk uses cookies to:

  • Ensure the website functions properly

Cookies do not collect personal information such as names, passwords, or payment details.

MANAGING COOKIES

You can choose to:
